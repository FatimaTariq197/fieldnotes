The percentages above are not a medical diagnosis. They only measure how many symptoms you selected overlap with patterns we've listed for common, general conditions — nothing here accounts for your full history, an exam, or test results, which is what an actual diagnosis requires. A high percentage doesn't mean "this is what you have," and a low one doesn't rule anything out. Only a clinician who can examine you can tell you what's really going on. If symptoms are severe, sudden, or getting worse, please seek in-person care rather than relying on this.
